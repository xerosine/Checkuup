Legal Disclaimer

CheckUup is a support and wellness app designed to foster community-driven mental health awareness. It is not a substitute for professional medical, psychiatric, or psychological advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The app does not provide emergency services or crisis intervention. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, experiencing a medical emergency, or facing a mental health crisis, please contact local emergency services immediately.

CheckUup provides behavioral tracking and check-in notifications based on user engagement data. While we strive for accuracy, we cannot guarantee the detection of all behavioral changes or mental health risks. Users are encouraged to seek professional support if needed.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. Our Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store information to ensure a secure and trustworthy experience.

  • Data Collection: We collect behavioral and engagement data to monitor trends and facilitate check-in notifications. We do not access private messages or personal conversations.
  • User Control: Users control their trusted circle and permission settings, including the option to allow emergency alerts.
  • Confidentiality: All personal data is encrypted and shared only with designated trusted contacts and, if permitted, emergency services.
  • Third-Party Partnerships: CheckUup partners with mental health organizations to provide resources and support but does not share personal data without explicit consent.
  • Account Management: Users can delete their accounts and request data removal at any time.
